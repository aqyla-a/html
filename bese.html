A photograph is a visual representation captured using a camera or other image-capturing devices. It records a moment in time, preserving scenes, people, objects, or events as a still image.

Photographs can be in black and white, sepia, or full color, and they may vary in style, such as portraits, landscapes, action shots, or abstract compositions. They are often used for artistic expression, documentation, communication, or as a means of storytelling. Photographs can be displayed physically (e.g., printed on paper) or digitally on screens and online platforms.
